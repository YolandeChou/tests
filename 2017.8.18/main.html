How to Make a Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

  1. Collect your ingredients

    • Two slices of bread
    • Peanut Butter
    • Jelly or Jam

  2. Spread peanut butter onto a piece of bread

    This will require a butter knife.

  3. Spread jelly or jam onto the other piece of bread

    This will also require a butter knife.

  4. Place bread slices together

    Make sure the peanut butter and jelly/jam sides are facing towards each other.

    Optional: You can slice the sandwich in half.

  5. Enjoy!
